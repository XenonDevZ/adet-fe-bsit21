## SLIDE 3 — Introduction
⏱️ *~50 seconds*

> "So, what is ACBS?
>
> The **Academic Consultation Booking System** is a web-based platform that we developed to streamline how students schedule and attend consultations with their teachers — all in one place.
>
> At its core, ACBS is a school-based system that manages the full consultation process — from the moment a student requests a session, all the way to the feedback given after it's done.
>
> It was built for **three types of users**: students, teachers, and school administrators — specifically for our community here at Liceo de Cagayan University.
>
> And the reason we built it? To replace the informal, scattered methods our school currently uses — and replace them with something **centralized, reliable, and real-time**."

---

## SLIDE 4 — Project Overview
⏱️ *~50 seconds*

> "Let me give you a quick overview of the system.
>
> ACBS supports **two consultation modes** — face-to-face and online. Students can browse a directory of teachers, see their available schedules, and book a session that fits their needs.
>
> On the teacher's side, they can manage their weekly availability, review incoming booking requests, and approve or decline them.
>
> Admins, on the other hand, have full visibility — they can manage all user accounts across the platform and monitor every consultation happening in the system.
>
> Key features include **live chat during sessions**, **Google Meet integration** for online consultations, **instant notifications**, **file sharing**, and even **reschedule requests** with teacher approval."

---

## SLIDE 5 — Problem Statement
⏱️ *~50 seconds*

> "But before we talk more about what ACBS does — let's talk about **why we built it**.
>
> Right now, most consultation arrangements in schools like ours are done informally. Students send messages, knock on teachers' doors, or make verbal agreements — and this leads to **scheduling conflicts, missed sessions, and a lot of confusion**.
>
> Communication is scattered across different platforms — Facebook Messenger, text messages, emails — with **no official record** tied to any consultation.
>
> And for school administrators? There's **zero visibility**. They have no way of knowing how many consultations are happening, which teachers are overloaded, or which students aren't getting the support they need.
>
> These are real problems — and ACBS is our answer to all of them."

---

## SLIDE 6 — Methodology
⏱️ *~1 minute 30 seconds*

> "So, how does ACBS solve these problems? Let me walk you through the system flow.
>
> *(Point to each step)*
>
> First — a student **signs in** using their school Google account. No new password needed.
>
> Then, they **browse the teacher directory** — they can search by name, department, or subject.
>
> Once they find the right teacher, they **select an available time slot** and submit a booking request.
>
> The teacher receives a notification and can **approve or decline** the request — with optional notes to the student.
>
> Once approved, both parties receive a confirmation. When the scheduled time arrives, a **live chat window opens automatically** — no switching to another app. For online sessions, a **Google Meet link** is automatically generated and shared in the chat.
>
> After the session is done, both the student and the teacher can **leave feedback and a rating**.
>
> And throughout the entire process — every update triggers an **instant notification** for both parties. Students can also **request a reschedule** if something comes up, and teachers respond with a single action.
>
> The system handles all of this in one place — clean, structured, and real-time."

---

## SLIDE 7 — Conclusion
⏱️ *~30 seconds*

> "To wrap up —
>
> ACBS transforms what used to be a messy, informal process into a **smooth and structured experience** for everyone involved.
>
> Students get easy booking, live chat, and clear status tracking. Teachers get organized schedules and a professional consultation space. And the school gets full visibility and accountability.
>
> In short — ACBS makes academic support **accessible, accountable, and effective**.
>
> Thank you! And now, let us show you the system in action."

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Practice once before** | Run through the whole script at least one time |
| **Don't read it word-for-word** | Use it as a guide — speak naturally |

| **Time yourself** | Use a phone timer during practice |

| **Avoid filler words** | No "uhm", "ah", "like" — pause instead |
